Our Work

OurWork
We’ve worked with clients from all over the globe, hailing from a variety of industries. Take a look at some of the industries we have worked with in the past.
OurWork

E-Commerce

E-Commerce is unavoidable in today’s world. If you’re not selling and promoting your products via mobile apps or via the web you’re probably missing out on a huge potential customer base.

Apps can help you push messages to your customers, informing them of deals, sales, news and other offers. Apps also allow you to provide a more personalised experience to your customers.

With more and more web browsing and shopping occurring on smartphones instead of PC’s, a mobile optimised website or app is crucial for your business.

We can develop tailored mobile apps and websites to help you tap into new customer bases as well as reinforcing existing ones.

E-Services

People are now looking to the web and mobile apps to help sort out various aspects of their daily lives. Booking in a cleaning service for the apartment, arranging a pet grooming service and gardening are only a few examples of services that are now available via apps or mobile sites.

In today’s fast paced world, patience is a rare commodity. Mobile based services allow customers to quickly book or arrange a service on the go at a moment’s notice without waiting on hold on the phone to speak to a customer service rep.

Get your business transacting with customers by providing a cutting edge and efficient means through which they can do business with you.

Social

In our world of ever increasing connectivity, social apps are growing in popularity due to their ability to connect the masses.

Apps in this category can encompass everything from chatting to dating to photo sharing.

Social media gives people, regardless of their level of technical ability, a simple way to spread information to and connect with the masses. Media of this nature distinguishes itself from other forms of media due to its ease of access and immediacy.

This is truly a burgeoning space with new ways to connect with people in a creative fashion being continually innovated.

Location Based Services

Apps that function based on a given user’s current location have the ability to provide opportunities and information most relevant to a user based on their position.

The convenience of these services is becoming increasingly apparent through the proliferation of location based apps in recent times.

Want to entice nearby customers with limited offers at your store? Or find out which of your friends are nearby and available for a drink at the bar?

Location based services gives you the power to turn these kinds of ideas into a reality.
