Consulting

What is
Lokava Consulting

You might be wondering why you need to go through the consulting process.

In order to best answer this question, consider the following:

Would you rather spend $20,000 building an app that doesn’t live up to your expectations?

OR

Go through our consulting process, determine EXACTLY how an app will boost your business and finally… build it within YOUR budget?

Consulting in its essence involves us talking with you about your idea and getting to the crux of what it is you’re trying to do. What problem you’re looking to solve. What need you’re looking to fill. We’ll talk and then talk some more…and some more until we’ve figured out all the nitty gritty details.

But the rewards are well worth it. An app is an investment in your business and we want to make sure that you get the largest return on investment possible!

Process

How does the Process work?

When you go through the Lokava Consulting process, we’ll discuss in depth:

1. What your idea is

2. The idea validation process – why is your idea a good solution

3. Existing competitors in the industry

4. Monetization strategies

5. Marketing strategies

6. Choosing a platform (iOS, Android or Web)

7. Building an MVP

-1-

Completely fresh, new ideas are hard to come by these days.

Most products are slight tweaks or variations on existing products and services.

Every now and then you’ll come across a brilliant and unique product and wonder how no one ever thought of it before.

Whichever case you are, we’ll discuss your idea in-depth and where it stands.

-2-

Idea validation is crucial. What we discuss here can either make or break your idea.

We’ll go in depth and talk about any idea validation you’ve done.

If you haven’t gone about validating your idea, we’ll give you an in-depth overview on validation strategies and how to go about it as efficiently as possible.

-3-

Always look to the competition. Always!

It’s the best way to learn what is being done right and what is being done wrong.

Even looking at the social media pages of a competitor’s product should give you an insightful look into what user base think of them and what they offer.

-4-

How do you make money with your app?

Any successful app launch with a wide user base is great but if you want your app to make you money, you need a monetization strategy.

We can discuss various monetization models with you so that you’re making the most of your investment and turning a healthy profit.

-5-

Marketing your app basically involves getting as many of the right people using your app as possible.

We’ll figure out the best marketing strategy for your app, based on your app industry, target demographic and various other factors.

-6-

Platform choice can be tough.

How do you know which is the best to pick?

We’ll work with you to figure out which platform you should look to launch on first.

Once the initial launch is successful, we can discuss a strategy and roadmap for launches on the other major platforms.

-7-

Building an MVP will be the culmination of all of the previously mentioned steps. The MVP or Minimum Viable Product is the ‘lowest investment possible’ version of your app needed to his the market and generate maximum ROI (return on investment).

Building and releasing an MVP will allow you to test your assumptions and make informed decisions about the direction of your app. You may end up discarding a feature your users never really use and adding a different one instead. You might tweak the user interface of the app based on feedback received.

These are the sorts of insights you can get from the MVP process.

Results

Define the key features needed for your app to be successful

Initial app ideas are usually not fully developed. On the surface the idea might seem great but you will always discover oversights and other problems once you dig a little deeper.

Our consulting process will help you determine the key features for your app based on your business needs.

It will also ensure that the finished product has the ability to maximize your revenue or improve an inefficient business process.

Work within YOUR target budget to build an MVP

To maximize your return on investment we use a lean startup concept called MVP (Minimum Viable Product)

The MVP is essentially the ‘lowest investment possible’ version of your app needed to hit the market and generate maximum ROI (return on investment).

Our consulting process will help ensure that we work within your budget to maximize the ability of the MVP to deliver the results you want.

If the MVP is a success, we can get started on the next version of your app!

Go through the Consulting process, Build your App and Save Money
