PlatformsWe work on

iOS

iOS apps are generally faster to build, release and monetise compared with their Android counterparts. We build iOS apps natively using Swift and Objective-C. We’ll work with you to build and launch your iOS app onto the App Store for iPhones and iPads everywhere.

Android

The most widely used mobile OS in the world. Building for Android means you’ll have the widest reach and potential adoption rate with your app. We build Android apps natively using Java. We’ll help build and launch your very own Android app on to the Google Play Store for Android devices everywhere.

Web

Mobile internet usage now exceeds that of the PC. With the ubiquity of mobile devices today, ensure that your web application/site is optimised for mobile as there’s an exceedingly high chance that your customers will be looking at your site on their phones first. We build web apps using various server side technologies including PHP, JSP and .NET. We will bring your web application to life through great UI/UX design, utilising the latest in web standards and technologies.
