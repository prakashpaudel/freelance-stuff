About Us

AboutUs
Lokava is an app development company based in Sydney, Australia.
We specialise in building Android, iOS (iPhone & iPad) and Web apps.

“We love playing with the latest technology and are especially happy when we can use technology to build amazing products for our clients.”

WhoWe Are

“We’re a bunch of technical consultants, developers and designers who love building things.”

Remember the excitement you felt when playing with Lego, Play-doh or the sand box when you were a kid? That’s how we feel everyday when we are working on building something for you.

WhyWork With Lokava

Communication is Key

In order to best understand your requirements, we’ll have an initial in-depth discussion to get to the core of your app idea. Analysing your idea at a deep level will help us deliver the best possible solution for you.

Following this, we’ll correspond with you regularly to keep you on top of the development process. Great communication from the get-go is key to project success!

Don’t stress, we’ve got your back

Having operated a startup and working with small-medium sized business and entrepreneurs has shown us that everyone shares common concerns. Will my target audience understand how to use my app? Will the app scale with more users? Does the development team appreciate my vision? The list goes on.

As we mentioned before communication is key. We’re with you every step of the way and will keep you informed so that any issues that arise are dealt with swiftly!

Expertise with the latest technologies

Leave the code juggling to us! Our super talented development team has extensive experience with iOS (iPhone & iPad), Android and Web apps.

We’ll provide you with regular builds so that you can test the app on your own smartphone or tablet.

You’ll get to see your app evolving every step of the way and provide us with crucial feedback.

Build your app without breaking the bank

We have plenty of experience working with startups, entrepreneurs and small-medium sized businesses. We love to optimise and will help distill your idea to the core components – a crucial task if you want to build your app in a lean and efficient manner.

We offer very competitive rates to build apps on iOS, Android and the Web. If you want to test out your new idea, we’ll work with you to develop your very own MVP (Minimum Viable Product).

Do judge a book by its cover

People DO and WILL judge your app based on its appearance. Bad design can turn off users, reduce app usage, lead to uninstalls and eventually a loss of revenue.

Don’t feel embarrassed to proudly show off your app. Great user interface design can not only make an app easier to use, it can delight users and keep them coming back.

Remember, your app is a reflection of you and your brand.

Always in motion is the future …

Just like the world, your business is constantly changing and evolving. It is hard to see what could be coming around the corner.

This is why at Lokava we provide post app deployment support for all of our clients.

Rest assured that if you’re having problems after your app is delivered to you, we’re here to help.

Who areOur Clients

We deal with people from all over the globe. From the US, UK, Singapore and China just to name a few.

Our clients are people who run startups or small to medium sized companies.


People with amazing, savvy ideas but without the technical know-how to realise their vision.

That’s where we step in!


We’d love to jump on a call, discuss your ideas and see if we can help you. Alternatively, just drop us an email and we’ll get back to you within 24hrs.
