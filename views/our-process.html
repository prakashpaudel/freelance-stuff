Our Process

OurProcess
Our Process is designed to be as intuitive and efficient as possible. We’ll keep you informed every step of the way to ensure that your vision for your app becomes a reality.
OurProcess

1. Discuss your idea

We have initial discussions about your idea.

2. Requirements Document

We document the features and functionality of your app at a deep level.

3. Prototyping

We will produce mockups of your app before we begin the development process to verify that the app is 100% aligned with your vision.

4. Design

During this process we’ll get your feedback and hone in on the app designs to be used going forward.

5. Coding

The coding process begins. Using agile methodology, we will keep you constantly updated on our progress and you will be able to inspect ongoing updates and provide feedback as each stage is completed.

6. Completion

Once the app is ready we will help you launch it on the App Store/Play Store.

LET’SGET STARTED

Get in touch and we can start making your app idea into reality.
